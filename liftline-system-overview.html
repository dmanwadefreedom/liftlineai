The Problem

Your Reps Are Wasting 1.5 Days Per Week on Scheduling

Every day your wholesalers spend chasing meetings is a day they're not building relationships and generating revenue. The calendar is the bottleneck.

Email Back-and-Forth

Wholesalers spend 1 to 1.5 days every week emailing advisors just to book meetings. That is 78 selling days lost per year.

Phone Tag

Calling advisors means navigating receptionists, assistants, and voicemail loops. Most calls never reach the decision maker.

Mental Note Follow-Ups

Reps keep track of "call this advisor in 5 weeks" in their heads or scattered notes. Follow-ups slip through the cracks.

Geography-Blind Scheduling

Driving 40 minutes between meetings because scheduling has no awareness of advisor locations. Wasted windshield time, fewer meetings per day.

Appointments Fall Through

Advisors booked 2+ weeks out often do not know their own schedule yet. No confirmation means high no-show rates.

The Solution

LiftlineAI Scheduling System

Three intelligent layers working together to automate every step - from initial outreach to post-meeting follow-up.

1
The Core

Email Scheduler

  • Reads advisor database from Salesforce - zone, A/B/C tier, contact info
  • 2 weeks before a zone visit, auto-emails advisors to book meetings
  • Prioritizes: A advisors first, B second, C third, 1-2 prospects per week
  • Geography-aware - schedules by location to minimize drive time
  • All emails through Outlook for compliance - every message captured and auditable
  • Handles back-and-forth rescheduling automatically
2
Voice AI

Confirmation Caller

  • 24 hours before each meeting, AI calls the advisor to confirm
  • "Hi, this is [name], calling on behalf of [wholesaler] - just confirming your meeting tomorrow at [time]"
  • Handles rescheduling on the spot if the advisor needs to move the meeting
  • Updates calendar in real-time - rep always has the latest schedule
3
Close the Loop

Post-Meeting Follow-Up

  • Rep speaks notes after meeting: "Met with advisor, discussed Income Builder Fund and practice management"
  • AI generates a personalized follow-up email from the voice notes
  • Sends through Outlook - fully compliance-safe
  • Schedules next touchpoint based on advisor tier and cadence rules
